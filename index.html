Who Are We?

In such a politically influenced time, we noticed how easy it is for young people form strong opinions without fully understanding the systems behind them. We believe writing should not simply be a reaction to major headlines, but an effort to understand the actual context behind them. Global Ink provides young writers with a platform to publish their work and share thoughtful perspectives on global issues to help promote more intentional content within the world around us.
