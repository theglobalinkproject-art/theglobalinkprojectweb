From our Editor in Chief

Welcome to the very first issue of The Global Ink Literary. In our rapidly evolving world, so much is changing every day, and this issue, 'The World in Motion: Stories of Change and Progress,' explores and analyses these shifts. Through these works, we aim for youth to be more informed and inspired to explore their own new perspectives on various events. We look forward to future issues covering a wide range of topics.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give youth around the world a platform to write about the global events they care about. We publish essays, papers, and other literary works, giving young voices the opportunity to be read, heard, and recognized. Through this work, we aim to help youth stay informed about the events shaping our world, develop their own unique perspectives and opinions, and broaden their understanding of global issues.
